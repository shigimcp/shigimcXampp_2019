Hello My Lovelies!

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Paul Wharton.

For the last decade I’ve had the privilege of helping countless women discover how to look and feel their most stunning. I learned as a teenager that we’re not always dealt a fair hand when it comes to problem skin but with the proper tools and products, flawless skin can be every person’s reality.

Throughout my journey of beauty discovery, it became clear to me that the key to good looks lies in the health and appearance of our skin. I didn’t have to shout this from a mountain top because as luck would have it, I was blessed to reach millions of people year after year as a Television Beauty and Style Expert.

It all started when I made an everyday young woman into a Supermodel on "Made", the Emmy winning show that I hosted with MTV back in 2002. Over the next decade, I went on to do makeovers on shows like TV Land’s "She’s Got the Look", TV One’s "Makeover Manor", Vh-1's "The Agency" and the list goes on. I even partnered with Elle Girl Magazine to host an online makeover series entitled "Go See with Paul Wharton" and for the past two seasons I’ve shared my makeover tips on my own show "Paul Wharton Style"!

When it came time to develop my own line of skin care products, I came to the table with my decade of makeover, product and beauty knowledge and with my friend and esteemed plastic surgeon, Dr. Paul Ruff IV we developed Conquer Beauty.

I am so proud to present you with the highest level of quality products that are safe, natural and revolutionary in their formulations. I believe in the rejuvenating power of my products and so I say to you...
